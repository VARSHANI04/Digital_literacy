📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the CSE0001 – Digital Literacy course. The aim of this project is to develop essential digital skills required for students in today’s technology-driven world.
As a Student Digital Ambassador, this project focuses on creating awareness about digital literacy, building a professional online presence, exploring useful platforms, understanding email etiquette, and learning about cyber safety.
The project is divided into five tasks, each focusing on a specific area of digital learning and practical application.

🧩 Task Summary

📊 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
In this task, I created an infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. The design includes topics like safe internet usage, digital tools, and professional online behavior.
This task helped improve my creativity and ability to present information visually.

🌐 Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
I created and updated my profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and others. This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional online presence and how these platforms can be used for learning and career growth.

💻 Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
I explored coding platforms by solving a beginner-level problem and created a Google Form quiz for digital literacy awareness. This task helped me understand how online tools can be used for practice, collaboration, and data collection.

📧 Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Communication
In this task, I wrote professional emails and created a checklist for responsible social media usage. It helped me learn how to communicate effectively and professionally in digital environments.

🔐 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
I studied a cybercrime case and created a prevention checklist. This task increased my awareness about online threats and taught me how to stay safe in the digital world.
